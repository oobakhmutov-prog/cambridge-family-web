# CONTENT_STRUCTURE.md

## Goal

Preserve and organize the content structure of the current website while making it easier to maintain and scale.

## Main UX principle

The website should support both:
- users ready to choose a route immediately
- users who want to understand the full product first

Because of that, the homepage should use soft routing.

## Suggested top-level pages

- Home
- Kindergarten
- School
- About
- Space / Environment
- FAQ
- Contact

## Homepage role

The homepage should:
- explain the full product clearly
- present the unified value proposition
- introduce kindergarten and school paths early
- continue selling the product for undecided users
- lead users toward a consultation / tour / inquiry

## Homepage structure

Possible structure:
1. Hero
2. Programs / routes overview
3. Why Cambridge Family
4. Educational environment / values
5. Space / gallery preview
6. How admission or first contact works
7. Testimonials / trust
8. FAQ
9. Final CTA

## Route pages

### Kindergarten page
Should answer:
- who it is for
- age groups
- daily experience
- benefits
- environment
- parent concerns
- next step

### School page
Should answer:
- who it is for
- grades/stages
- educational approach
- environment
- parent concerns
- next step

## CTA strategy

Primary CTA:
- book a tour / request consultation / apply inquiry

Secondary CTA:
- learn more

CTA logic should stay consistent across pages.

## Content principles

- clear
- warm
- premium
- not overloaded
- easy to scan
- reassuring
- trust-building

## Avoid

- content bloat
- repeated blocks with little value
- mixing all programs too deeply on the homepage
- unclear navigation labels
